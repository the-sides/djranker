Welcome to DJ Ranker

Imagine a live party playlist where everyone has a voice. Songs may be searched and requested by anyone with a link at anytime from a single page.

No signup necessary.

Every request will have score and will be open to be voted on. If your music sucks, thanks to everyone else, we won't have to deal with it.
At -3 points, a requested song will be removed. As requests score higher than others, the queue is changed while the songs ranked highest are played next off the top.

RULES:

  1. The host must have a Premium Spotify account.
  2. The song after the current will be locked in order for the program to work. Blame Spotify.
  3. The host must not play in shuffled mode and for the time-being, cannot skip songs. Refrain from touching the Spotify App as much as possible with the except for volume.

FEATURES:

  1. Current Song Display: No more asking what song is playing.
  2. Volume Adjust Request: Too loud? Not bumping enough? Vistors can anonomously request that a volume change is made in either direction and the host is able to the stats for either. Doesn't matter they'll do anything about it. Yes, I made this for you Mikey.
  3. History and Playlist Link: Do you ever hear that song that comes up the following day which just won't get out of your head? Significantly worse when you didn't know the song to start with. Take home the playlist with a history lasting 24 hours and a playlist to all the music that was played.
